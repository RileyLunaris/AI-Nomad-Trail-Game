# AI Nomad Trail Game
A modern, AI-powered reimagining of the classic Oregon Trail game.

Instead of traveling west in a wagon, players journey from across the united states, managing a limited budget while navigating unpredictable events inspired by real-world news and AI-generated scenarios.

## Game Overview
In this game, players will:
- Travel across the United States
- Manage their budget-based prompts
- Make strategic decisions when unexpected events occur
- Adapt to situations inspired by current news articles and AI-generated prompts


## How It Works
- **AI-Generated Events**  
  Events are created using AI prompts to introduce unique, story-driven challenges and decisions.

- **News-Based Scenarios**  
  Real news articles are scraped from the web and transformed into in-game events, grounding gameplay in real-world context.

- **Decision-Driven Gameplay**  
  Player choices directly impact:
  - Budget
  - Progress
  - Outcome of the journey


## Features
- Cross-country travel gameplay
- Budget and resource management
- Real-world news integration
- AI-driven dynamic events
- High replayability with unique runs
